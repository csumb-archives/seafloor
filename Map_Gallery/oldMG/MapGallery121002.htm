MAP GALLERY

Example imagery from various multibeam survey projects from the western United States (California and Washington coasts) and Antarctica. Full extent and closeup views of shaded relief grayscale and depth-colored multibeam bathymetry are included. Many of these data are displayed in this websites IMS viewer.

Please note that these images are for example purposes only and have been compressed for display on this website. Note, the imagery displayed here is not to be used for navigation. Please contact Pat Iampietro at the SFML if you are interested in obtaining the original images or associated data.
