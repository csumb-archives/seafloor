Map Gallery

Example imagery from various multibeam survey projects produced through the Sea Floor Mapping Lab. Full extent and closeup views of shaded relief grayscale and depth-colored multibeam bathymetry are included in a layout format. Many of these data are displayed in this websites IMS viewer.

Note, the imagery displayed here is not to be used for navigation. Please contact Pat Iampietro at the SFML if you are interested in obtaining the original images or associated data.
