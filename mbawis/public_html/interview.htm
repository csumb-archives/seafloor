**Interviewing Skills for Scientists**

Sponsored by Clontech, www.clontech.com *******************************************************

Worried about the dreaded interview? Want to learn about good interviewing skills from actual interviewers? Science's Next Wave (www.nextwave.org) will be presenting our "Interviewing Skills for Scientists" program on February 19 during the annual AAAS meeting at the Hilton San Francisco and Towers. The workshop will begin with a mock interview, demonstrating both "good" and "poor" interviewing skills. We will have an expert panel (composed of scientists from industry and other careers) to provide comments on the mock interview as well as offer advice on what makes a candidate shine. Before we break for a reception, a human resources (HR) professional will highlight what to expect in the HR portion of a job interview, and you'll get to ask all of our experts your questions about the interview process.

This workshop occurs directly following the Science Career Fair (10AM-4PM). Throughout the day, there are other career workshops. For a small entrance fee of $10 for AAAS members and $25 for non-members, you can attend all of these workshops.

For more information about the Career Fair and the career workshops, please go to our Events Calendar at http://nextwave.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/full/2000/03/17/1.
