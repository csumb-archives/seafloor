The Monterey Bay Chapter of AWIS had its first organizational meeting on August 22, 1998.

Participants at that meeting expressed a variety of ideas concerning Chapter goals and programs, including a strong interest in community outreach to school children and the non-science public.

Our Chapter hopes to embody a diverse membership representing various disciplines and sectors of work, including: academia, industry, NGO's, government agencies, science education, public education and journalism.

Our previous electronic newsletter has been replaced by this web site. Please browse this site for current information on monthly meetings and scheduled events. Our Chapter meetings are open to all those who are interested in attending.
