MONTEREY BAY CHAPTER

It is time for us to elect a New Executive Board for Monterey Bay AWIS.

This is an especially exciting time for someone to join the Executive Board. The Monterey Bay Chapter (MB-AWIS) is established and an active part of NCC (the funding arm of the Northern California Chapters of AWIS). All that nitty-gritty work formulating by-laws, setting up books and banking accounts, establishing listservs, developing a web site, etc. -has been done. Recent outreach activities have been successful and with a bit of organized follow-up MB-AWIS could begin to grow rapidly from its base. It is the perfect time of people with excitement, energy, and the desire to take the next steps to come forward and serve on the Executive Board and as Committee Chairs.

The Executive Board has five voting members; President, President-Elect, Secretary, Treasurer, and Member at Large. The non-voting members are the NCC-AWIS liaison and the Chairs of all the Committees (Programs, Membership, Outreach, etc).

If you are interested in taking this opportunity to be a major influence in the direction of MB-AWIS over the next several years please contact someone on the current Executive Board. They can answer any questions you have about participating in a rewarding program like AWIS. Please contact President Carrie Bretz Carrie_Bretz@monterey.edu, Treasurer Victoria Welborn (welborn@cats.ucsc.edu), Member-at-large Jill Zande (jzande@marinetech.org), Secretary Meghan Kinney (meghan.kinney@doj.ca.gov), or Membership Chair Julie Doerr (julie.doerr@doj.ca.gov).

Terms of office begin October 1 so please contact us soon so we will have ample time to turnover materials to the new Board this summer/fall and let the new Board establish committees, etc. thus being able to begin a year of events in Fall. We anticipate a smooth transition for the new members. The current Board consists of people who have served two (or more) terms in their positions so are unable to be considered for re-election.

I encourage you to peruse this web site to see the types of events and programs that have been implemented over the past years.

So, if you want to participate in programs that could impact the future of local women in the sciences, get involved in the Monterey Bay Chapter of AWIS today!

Cheers!

Carrie Bretz
