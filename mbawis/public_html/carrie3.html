*The NCC-AWIS committee: serves the members of the Northern California chapters of AWIS (East Bay, Monterey Bay, Palo Alto, Sacramento Valley) through representation on the regional issues in the community and through the organization of fund-raising, awards presentation, workshops and conferences of interest to all members. Fundraising efforts help support local projects such as outreach, mentoring, and awarding local scholarships.
