Monterey Bay Chapter membership:

Benefits Your membership dues will help promote and manage science seminars, special events and workshops, outreach programs for women scientists and students in our local Monterey Bay region, as well as provide a networking link to the other (3) Northern California Chapters. We encourage members to participate in the development of all programs by attending bi-monthly board meetings.

How Please complete the form below & send (with your check) to: MB-AWIS Membership, c/o Julie Doerr, 440 Airport Blvd., Bldg. A Watsonville, CA 95076, (or c/o Carrie Bretz, CSUMB, 100 Campus Center, Seaside, CA 93955). Annual Monterey Bay Chapter dues are $5 for students, $10 for non-students.

Both Monterey Bay Chapter & National membership:

Benefits Associated membership with National will allow local members the ability to participate on the Monterey Bay Chapter Executive Board and have full voting priviledges. See Constitution & Bylaws on our web site for further details. National AWIS members are entitled to partipate in several nationally-focused programs from scholarships to internships, from publications to mentoring. You will also receive the quarterly AWIS Magazine.

How 1) Preferred: Checks for both National & Monterey Bay Chapter dues can be sent to the address given above. The information will then be sent to National through the Monterey Bay Chapter Membership Chair. Annual dues for the National program are on a sliding-scale based on your income ($20-$60). Details can be found on the National AWIS web site (www.awis.org). 2). Or: you can join AWIS on-line using a credit card at www.awis.org/html/membership.html by completing the form for both Monterey Bay Chapter and National membership.
