Moss Landing Marine Laboratories

Directions

From the North: Hwy 1 south to Moss Landing. Take first right (towards the ocean) onto Moss Landing Road- after you pass the PGE power stacks (on the left/east). The exit road is BEFORE the pink Whole Enchilada restaurant. Follow Moss Landing Road around past the antique stores and post office. Do not take the right towards the harbor or cross the bridge. Stay on Moss Landing road. If you pass the cemetery, you went a bit too far. There is a sign for the marine lab at the bottom of a long driveway. The building is not visible from the road. Go up the driveway and park in the lot, then follow the winding sidewalk to the front doors. The meeting room is on the left near the library.
