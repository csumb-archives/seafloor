Our Chapter serves women in science-related fields in the Monterey Bay region. We hope the Monterey Bay Chapter will represent the diverse cross-section of women in various career stages, scientific disciplines and science education programs, and develop a platform for women to network among these various groups. Because of this diverse membership, we would like the Chapter aims to emphasize the issues common to all women in science as well address the interests of specific science disciplines.

 The purpose of the questionnaire is to help us (and you) better assess the interests and needs of women in the local science community.
