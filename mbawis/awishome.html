MONTEREY BAY CHAPTER

Dear Members,

I am sad to announce that the Monterey Bay Chapter is officially inactive. Due to the shortage of new volunteers to the executive board positions we were unable to organize new events or programs. I am hopeful that the Chapter will be resurrected in the near future. Feel free to contact me with ideas, especially if you are interested in reviving the Chapter. I would be most willing to help you get started and would serve as a board member. Please contact me.

The other Northern California Chapters (Palo Alto, East Bay, Sacramento Valley) have active memberships and programs, so please contact them if you want to get involved with AWIS and network with other local women in science.

Cheers!
