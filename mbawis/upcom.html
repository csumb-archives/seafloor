MONTEREY BAY ASSOCIATION FOR WOMEN IN SCIENCE

Applications must be postmarked by March 15 March 22

The Monterey Bay Chapter of the Association for Women in Science (AWIS) is pleased to announce its annual scholarship for women pursuing a science degree at one of the following local community colleges: Cabrillo, Gavilan, Hartnell, and Monterey Peninsula. Each college will have one recipient who will receive $250.

Professors at these institutions are asked to nominate students who they believe have excelled in their classes. This may be accomplished simply by providing students with an application and writing a letter of support. We highly encourage re-entry students to apply for this award. Application forms (PDF format) may be downloaded from this Monterey Bay Chapter web site- http://skyler.monterey.edu/~mbawis

A COMPLETE APPLICATION INCLUDES:

  • A Completed Application Form (available on-line)
  • Transcript(s) of your high school (if you graduated in the last 5 years) or college record. The transcript may be a copy (it is not necessary to send a sealed original). Please include the courses you are currently enrolled in.
  • A letter of nomination from a science professor
  • An Essay- In one typed page, please tell us why you should be awarded this scholarship. Describe your school experience, your career plans and goals in the field of science.
