MiniROV System-
SeaBotix LBV 200L

The SeaBotix MiniROV is a portable, compact, yet powerful system Combined with our sonar technology


Features:
LBV
* 200 meter depth rating
* 21in(L)x9.6in(W)x10in(H)
Thrusters
* 4 powerful Brushless DC thrusters
* Max surface speed 3 knts
Integrated Control System
* Integrated control console
* Intuitive hand controller/joystick
* 38 cm color LCD monitor
* Sunshade cover
* Auto depth, heading & trim
Umbillical
* 250 meters of cable
* Rugged, easy spool umbilical reel
Camera & Lighting
* Fiber optic video
* FSK electronics
* 270º field of view
* Hi-Res color camera
* Low light B/W camera
* High intensity LED array (tracking color camera)
* Video overlay

*System specs taken from the SeaBotix web site
