The Seafloor Mapping Lab (SFML) of California State University Monterey Bay is no longer an active research mapping program. The SFML Data Library, housing an extensive archive of multibeam bathymetry data, derived products, and metadata for coastal California, will remain publicly available through this website. 07/2018
