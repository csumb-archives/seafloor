National Sea Grant College Program

Fisheries Habitat Characterization of the California Continental Margin: Identification, Quantification, and Synthesis of Existing Information

National Sea Grant College Program Project #R/F-181A

This project constitutes the initial stages of building both a database and a series of habitat maps that can be used to plan and implement effective Essential Fish Habitat programs for California. Managers and fishery scientists can now identify data voids and more capably define areas for future studies.
