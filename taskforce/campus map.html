Directions to the Task Force Meeting at the CSU-Monterey Bay University Center:

Exit Highway 1 at CSUMB/Fort Ord Main Entrance. Turn left on North South Road. Turn right at the second stop sign (Third Street). Turn right at the second stop sign (Sixth Avenue). The University Center is a couple of blocks down on the right hand side, and it has a white domed circus tent like top (you can't miss it). There should be signs guiding you to the meeting. Attached is a PDF map of campus. The meeting is in building 29.
