Hotel Directions:

Monterey Beach Hotel

Traveling on Highway 1, take exit 218 (Del Rey Oaks). The Monterey Beach Hotel is located 1/2 block west of Highway 1.

Map

Embassy Suites Hotel

Exit Highway 1 at Highway 218/Seaside Exit to Canyon del Ray. Travel east on Canyon del Rey to first stop light. Hotel is located at intersection of Canyon del Rey and Del Monte Blvd. (giant pink building on the Monterey Peninsula... hard to miss).
